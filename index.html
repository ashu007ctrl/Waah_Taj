About

A Monument of Eternal Love

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, is an ivory-white marble mausoleum commissioned in 1632 by Emperor Shah Jahan to house the tomb of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. A UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1983.
